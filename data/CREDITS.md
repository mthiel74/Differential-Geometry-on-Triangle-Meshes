# Data credits & provenance

Almost everything under `data/` is generated by the project's own Wolfram
Language pipeline (test meshes, Wolfram-model graphs, numeric snapshots) and
carries no third-party rights. The one external asset is the 3D scan used in
§8 ("the operators on a real 3D scan").

## `trex.obj`, `trex_coarse.obj` — Tyrannosaurus rex skull

Decimated (via `scripts/prepare_trex.py`) from the photogrammetric scan:

- **Title:** *Vertebrate: Tyrannosaurus rex skull (MOTE)* — a museum cast on
  display at the Museum of the Earth, Ithaca, New York.
- **Author:** Emily Hauf
- **Publisher:** Digital Atlas of Ancient Life / Paleontological Research
  Institution (PRI), Ithaca, New York.
- **License:** **CC0 1.0** (Public Domain Dedication) — no rights reserved.
- **Source:** https://sketchfab.com/3d-models/vertebrate-tyrannosaurus-rex-skull-mote-cf48487de55d454b8adc92dce2840a0d

CC0 imposes no conditions, but we credit the creators as a courtesy and to
record provenance. The committed `trex*.obj` are welded, largest-component,
quadric-decimated, consistently-oriented and unit-normalised derivatives of the
above; being CC0, these derivatives are likewise unrestricted.
